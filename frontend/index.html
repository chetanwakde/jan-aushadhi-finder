About Jan Aushadhi
Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) is a Government of India initiative to provide quality generic medicines at affordable prices through dedicated Jan Aushadhi Kendras.
Key Facts
✅ Generic medicines are bioequivalent to branded
✅ Manufactured by WHO-GMP certified plants
✅ Saves patients ₹7,500+ crore annually
✅ 10,500+ Jan Aushadhi Kendras across India
✅ 2,000+ medicines available under PMBJP
